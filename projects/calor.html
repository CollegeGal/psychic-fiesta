Calor

Calor Magazine presents the story of a sustainable skincare brand dedicated to continuing its journey of preserving the planet. This fictional brand forges its own path, a journey that this issue guides its readers through. Dive into an exploration of eco-friendly practices and innovative strategies that define the brand's commitment to sustainability and beauty. Join us as we uncover the essence of caring for the earth while nurturing your skin.
